Who am I

I am a UI/UX Designer that loves that which is unique and who believes that small actions can have a world wide impact.
I specialize in planning, creativity and Design Thinking / Lean Startup based project development.

What I enjoy

I love developing projects of all sizes, from design and templating to iteration improvement of both web applications, branding and product and systems creation.
I put special care in designing user experiences that are simple, lovely and that have a huge impact.
I enjoy creating brand identity, icons and illustrations.

My Experience

My professional experience has centered around the development and implementation of design projects in the field of education.
I have experience as a teacher and educational processes guide based on the Design Thinking methodology.
I have a Masters by the UB (University of Barcelona) in Digital Content Management.

My Specialties

UI/UX, UX Research, Design Thinking, Trend Hunter, Graphic Design, Branding, Photography, Advertising photography, Art Direction.
